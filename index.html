Physicians' Diagnostic Services Center, Inc.

Physicians’ Diagnostic Services Center, Inc., established in 1979, is a leading diagnostic clinic specializing in health screenings for overseas workers. Accredited by the Department of Health (DOH), Department of Labor and Employment (DOLE), and endorsed by the Philippine Overseas Employment Administration (POEA), the clinic serves workers bound for Asia-Pacific, the Middle East, and Europe. It is also recognized by various maritime and immigration authorities across 28 countries. Staffed by experienced professionals, it offers a comprehensive range of medical screening services.

OUR SERVICES

We are committed to delivering high-quality, personalized healthcare.
